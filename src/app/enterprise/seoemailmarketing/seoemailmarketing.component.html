OUR PROCESS

SEO Audit01/07

  • Understand your Business, Target market and expected goals
  • Assess your site’s present performance – Content, Mapping, Codes and keywords volume
  • Draft the site’s shortcomings and positives

Objective and Strategy02/07

  • Set the objective and goals of the planned SEO Campaigns, Establish between teams and customer
  • Create a strategic plan to revamp your current website and enhance it achieve proposed results

Configure03/07

  • Keywords research with across topic covering keywords with high search volume, logical customer search pattern setup, Brainstorm sessions
  • Perform long-tail keyword research in order to form the content strategy for better search ranking. (Pillar Content curation of old and creation of new – Blogs, Articles, Guides, Whitepapers etc.)

Deploy04/07

  • Execution of on-site and on-page optimization strategies that consist of cleaning up your site’s code, content, architecture, mapping (Internal links etc.), keyword usage (Frontloading, Synonyms etc.), Content and Image Optimization, Page speed optimization, Landing Page Load Speed, Navigation or Crawlability in the webpage, URL Structure and Code Cleaning
  • Optimized the inventory of all the page URLs, current Meta Titles, Meta Descriptions, robot.txt, Sitemap and 404 errors. Also Search engine penalty recovery if required.

Employ05/07

  • Implementation of off-page optimization strategies which includes quality link building (Back Links), Building links through content marketing, SMO (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.), Blog Directories submissions (EatonWeb, Ontoplist, etc.), Engagement in Q&A forums, links to Social Bookmarking (StumbleUpon) etc.

Monitor06/07

  • Observer and evaluate the organic traffic, Keyword Rankings, Click-through rate (CTR), site’s position in SERPs, Visits per page, Scroll Depth, Decreased Bounce Rates, Pages per Visit, Time on Page, Conversion Rate (Any Action from signup to purchase)

Report and Analyze07/07

  • Summarize to check the results of the campaign, real time check on performance and milestones, constant follow-up with the customer with the report
  • On the go optimization after the Campaign starts to fill the inconsistencies
