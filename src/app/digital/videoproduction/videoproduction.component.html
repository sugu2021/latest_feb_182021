The more time you will spend on your planning, the smoother will be your film, and better will be your final video. Thus, this phase will ensure that your video is a success.     After pre-planning, this is the spans where actual filming starts. During this phase, the camera rolls, and all presentations, renditions, and overlay shots captured to video.
