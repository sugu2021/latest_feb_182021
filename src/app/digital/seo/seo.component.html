FAQ'S ON SEO



SEO services are charged monthly because of the lag in ever changing search engines algorithms. Changes made to any website take weeks to a few months to show effect on the search results and we move further after analyzing the impact. The activities can be divided into one time and recurring activities which have to be carried out month on month.

White-hate SEO services and strategies take at least 4 to12 months depending on the keyword difficulty (KD) to rank websites at the top. No credible SEO agency can provide a concrete timeline for results but can only promise definite results.

Highly competitive and generic target keywords take more than a year to beat the competition as the search algorithms change consistently and shift the rankings up or down to all the websites worldwide.

Optimizing your website for the first time will bring you to a higher ranking and drive traffic after at least 4-6 months after the campaign launch. Aged websites with high quality content can show the positive results in 2-3 months.

Once your webpages start receiving good traffic and ranking, we can work on other pages and relevant keywords or provide you with other SEO services. With dynamic changes on the web, we always have something new to offer, you decide when to stop.

Websites breathe through text as search engines cannot figure out what the web page is about unless there is a description that revolves around a keyword or topic. Without professional content guided by SEO experts, your website can never rank on top.

It is definitely a better option than traditional marketing. SEO services build the pillars of the digital assets every business must have.

Organic Traffic is free. Paid SEO services are not only costly but their time frame ends as soon as you stop paying. It brings you local and targeted traffic along with promising leads.

White-hat SEO services revolve around content. The prime focus while optimizing our website for top search results is to deliver the requirements of the user. White-hate SEO is a long term approach to optimization.

White hat SEO techniques focus on content quality. Creating quality content proves the search engines that your website uniquely displays a service and is genuinely liked by the users that visit it. White hat SEO services focus on researching and generating keywords around your brand and pushing regular and factual content.

Black-hat SEO services use hacks to ace the search engines engine results by exploiting any weaknesses in their algorithms. They are usually geared towards search bots first, rather than human readers and can be penalized by the search engine.

Google search algorithms are regularly updated to tackle Black-hat methods like Florida, which penalized keyword stuffing and Panda, which penalized content farms.
