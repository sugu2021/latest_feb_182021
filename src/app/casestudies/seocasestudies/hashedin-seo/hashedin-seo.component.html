Case Study SEO B2B Technology Company

Our client builds deep tech SaaS products and platforms. They are experts in Analytics, Machine Learning & Integrations, and have launched over 200+ products. Our client was missing out on a lot of traffic and leads due to lack digital reach. With our proven expertise in SEO services, we were able to give them desired results within a few months.

Challenges

It isn't easy to rank your site in the technology niche due to the high-competition. The road to digital success of our client wasn’t easy. You have to have a website with high domain authority to rank for some competitive keywords. Our client didn’t have any organic traffic on their website and paid campaign wasn’t a solution as a paid campaign doesn’t support organic reach.

To sum up, the challenges faced by our clients were:

- No organic traffic from the search engine
- Some pages were not appearing in the search results
- Were not getting any leads through search engine traffic

Solution

Upon analysing their website, we found out that Google did not index a lot of their pages. There was no organic traffic from the search engine, and they were not ranking for any relevant keywords of their industry. Their on-page, off-page and technical-SEO needed a new approach, so we built a new strategy for every one of them.

Here's all the thing we did to improve our client SEO performance:

  • - Optimized XML sitemap, site speed, robots.txt, and other such factors under Technical SEO
  • - Created some high-quality backlinks to increase the authority of the website
  • - Created new high-quality content for longtail keyword
  • - Optimized all the existing page of their website
  • - Made SEO optimized content on all the business vertical and segments
  • - Optimized title tag, primary keywords, the header tag, meta description and all the things that come under on-page optimization.

Results

Within a tight timeline of six months, we were able to give some amazing results. More than 150 keywords around business verticals and segments were ranking on the search engine.
