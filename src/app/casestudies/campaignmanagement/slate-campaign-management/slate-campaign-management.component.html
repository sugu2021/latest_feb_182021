BrandStory increases sales conversion by 30% in 3 months for an Accounting Firm
Case Study Campaign Management B2B

Our client has been doing remarkably well in their industry. However, this was not always the case. Our client struggled to gain leads through with all their paid channels. With the help of our expert team, we were able to enhance the digital reach of our client.

Challenges

Whether it is Facebook ads or Google AdWords, a wrong ad campaign with a high budget can turn into a disaster. You will end up having irrelevant traffic on your website, which will eventually leave within seconds. All your money will go in vain without any significant leads. The ongoing paid campaign of the client was not performing well in all the platforms. Our client was an accounting service start-up, and organic traffic was not a solution as it takes time to establish a digital site.

The primary challenges faced by our client were:

- Irrelevant clicks through paid ads
- Few leads through the paid campaign
- CPC was higher than the average
- Setup ads in different categories such as accounting and GST

Solution

After going through their old paid campaigns, we found out a lot of mistakes that were wasting their budget. The targeted audience was too broad and irrelevant. Their social media accounts and website needed some change. We discussed the changes with our web designers and social media marketing team and soon implemented it.

Here are all the things we did to enhance our client’s paid ads campaign:

  • - Improved the ad placements
  • - Made some changes in their site and social media accounts
  • - Made changes in our ad placements strategy based on regular reports

Results

We were given a tight timeline of 3 months to showcase our skills, and we were able to deliver the desired results within the timeframe.

With the paid campaign, the sales increased to 30% within the time frame of 3 months.
The reach through the paid campaign was around 30,00,000.

Impact

Being a start-up, our client gained leads within a span of three months through the paid campaign. We built trust and awareness among users and generated leads within the time frame of three months.

We were able to impact our client’s business in the following ways:

  • - The sales increased by 30% within three months
  • - The Cost per Lead was around 70 INR – 100 INR
  • - Generated qualified leads through the campaign
  • - The ads hit a reach of 30,00,000
